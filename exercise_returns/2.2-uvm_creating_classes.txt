1: What is the responsibility of the monitor in UVM environment?

Observe the signals going in and out of the DUT

2: Why do we create the monitor in the agent and not directly in the environment?

Agent file has all the DUT related component handlers for the sake of reusability; therefore we create the monitor in the agent. 

3: What is the reason for extending your monitor from uvm_monitor base class instead of declaring the class yourself?

Extending uvm_monitor provides in built uvm functionalities such as phase execution, debugging and factory registration(no direct call to constructor).

4: What is the difference between tasks and functions in SystemVerilog? Why is the run_phase method a task, when other phase methods seem to be functions?

Tasks contains delays whereas functions do not contain delays.
 
Other phases (like build_phase, connect_phase) generally execute a sequence of actions that happen once and do not need to wait for time-based events. 
They typically contain configurations or object creation, making them appropriate for functions.
The run_phase requires an ongoing loop with event waiting and time-controlled operations, making it appropriate to implement as a task.
