1: What is the responsibility of the coverage collector in UVM environment?
A coverage collector utilizes SystemVerilog coverage constructs to inspect incoming transactions and record coverage statistics.


2: What is a covergroup? 
Coverage collectors in UVM work with covergroups that define a collection of variables we wish to have statistics on.
Covergroups consist of coverpoints which are broken down into bins.


3: Why did we declare the coverage collector functionality as write() method instead of run_phase task?
The write function gives the coverage report of the analysed transcation immidiately without waiting for the execution command. 


4: What kind of coverage data did you see in the coverage report?
Coverpoints of following:
c_data_to_DUT
c_data_from_DUT
